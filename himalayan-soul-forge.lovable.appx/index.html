6-Day Itinerary

A journey marked in dawns.

1

Day 1

Delhi → Pithoragarh

Approx 460 km, 10–11 hours. Evening local sightseeing and rest.
2

Day 2

Pithoragarh → Dharchula → Nabi Village

Complete permit process and enter the Himalayan route.
3

Day 3

 Nabi Village → Adi Kailash → Kalapani

Visit Nabhi village and witness the Adi Kailash region.
4

Day 4

Kalapani → Om Parvat Darshan → Pithoragarh

Sacred Om Parvat darshan and surrounding exploration.
5

Day 5

Pithoragarh → Jageshwar Dham & Almora

Begin the journey back through the Himalayan valleys.
6

Day 6

Almora → Kainchi Dham → Delhi

Travel back with memories that will outlast the mountains.
